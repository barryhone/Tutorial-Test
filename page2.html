ABOUT

This summer, the RadioActivists present, RM+JLT, a futuristic take on Romeo & Juliet that fuses modern, grimey, Irish hip hop in a dystopian future where the world has succumbed to climate disaster.


Introducing eco friendly, alternative nightlife to Dublin, the performance will be a completely sustainable, carbon neutral event. This unique cultural event, billed as a dystopian beach party promises love, beats and the apocalypse.

VENUE



THE MC's



ECO FOOTPRINT


Set on Sandymount Beach, beneath the shadow of the Poolbeg incinerator, this unique, pop up venue is ideal for a party at the end of the world.

Showcasing some of Ireland’s best MC’s today, Irish rap has never been so hard hitting and so elequent. The skills of Rasha Dose as JLT and Sun E Jim as RM, will have Shakespeare bouncing in his grave.

Throughout the performance, all sound and lighting will be powered by electric cars on sight. Only a 45 min walk from the city centre we encourage all attendees to travel conscientiously. No Plastic allowed!
